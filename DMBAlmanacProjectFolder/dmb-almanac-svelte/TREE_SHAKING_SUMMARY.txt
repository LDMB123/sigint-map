  └─ Used in: All visualization components

  └─ extent, max, min, mean
  └─ Used in: GapTimeline, SongHeatmap, RarityScorecard

  └─ axisLeft, axisRight, axisTop, axisBottom
  └─ Used in: GapTimeline, SongHeatmap, RarityScorecard

  └─ schemeCategory10, schemeBlues, schemeGreens, schemeReds, schemePurples

  └─ geoAlbersUsa, geoPath
