File: src/lib/components/ui/Tooltip.svelte

  - Modify: popovertargetaction from 'toggle' to 'show'
  - Benefit: Cleaner code, native browser behavior
  - Risk: LOW (Popover API stable, Chrome 114+)

Effort: 20 minutes

  ✅ Chrome 114+

  ✅ Firefox 125+
