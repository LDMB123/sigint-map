## Template Categories
- **home**: primary entry experience with stats and CTAs
- **list**: large datasets with filters/sort/virtualization
- **detail**: entity detail pages with metadata + related sections
- **content**: mostly static or editorial pages
- **utility**: system/PWA/diagnostics pages
