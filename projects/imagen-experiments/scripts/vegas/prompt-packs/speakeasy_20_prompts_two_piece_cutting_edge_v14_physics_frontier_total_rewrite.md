# Speakeasy Prompt Pack v14 - Physics Frontier Total Rewrite

## Why This Exists
This is a complete rewrite of all 20 prompts from the audits, built around physics-first prompting instead of texture-only prompting.

## System Design
Each prompt now uses an explicit simulation-style contract:
- Identity Constraint: preserve face geometry and expression asymmetry from reference.
- Gaze Constraint: enforce lens-axis eye contact with coherent catchlights.
- Domain Constraint: force commercial nightlife context with required scene anchors.
- Wardrobe Constraint: full replacement into a true two-piece cocktail topology.
- Mechanics Constraint: force-load causality from posture to folds to highlights to shadows.
- Safety Constraint: non-explicit editorial framing with stable coverage-critical edges.

## QA Gates (Hard)
Reject and regenerate if any gate fails:
1. Identity drift from reference.
2. Off-camera gaze.
3. Missing or incorrect two-piece transformation.
4. Missing scene anchors.
5. Broken physics chain (fake folds, detached highlights, impossible contact shadows).
6. Any explicit framing or unstable coverage-critical boundary.

## Prompt Authoring Standard
- Adult subject only (25+).
- Vertical 4:5 composition, full outfit legibility, realistic lens behavior.
- Commercial nightlife only; no home or bedroom substitutions.
- Preserve editorial edge through physically plausible styling, not explicit framing.
