## Prompt 04 - Onyx Atrium Boundary Layer

### Primary Prompt

MISSION:
Create a photoreal nightlife editorial image in a dry stone atrium with an illuminated onyx-brass feature wall at night. The output must feel like premium campaign photography, never synthetic artwork.

IDENTITY + GAZE LOCK:
- Use reference image only for the same adult woman (25+).
- Preserve face geometry: orbital spacing, brow architecture, nose bridge, lip contour, jawline, and natural asymmetry.
- Keep strict lens-axis eye contact with physically plausible vergence and coherent catchlights.
- Hard fail if identity drifts or gaze leaves lens axis.

SCENE DOMAIN LOCK:
- Required visible anchors: illuminated onyx-brass feature wall, dry stone walkway, brass handrail, lantern pair.
- Keep upscale commercial speakeasy environment only.
- Never introduce home/bedroom/residential elements.
- Keep scene strictly dry with clear-air conditions and no liquid/vapor atmospherics.

WARDROBE TOPOLOGY LOCK:
- Full replacement attire: plum structured two-piece set with sculpted top and moderated wrap slit skirt, black sheer hosiery, plum heels.
- Enforce true two-piece topology: separate top + separate skirt with visible waist boundary.
- Prohibit one-piece merges, bodysuit fallback, denim/casual carryover, or unresolved garment boundaries.

POSE + STORY:
- Pose directive: standing at atrium edge with one hand on brass rail and balanced long-line stance to camera.
- Narrative directive: post-peak calm with controlled confidence.
- Expression must read confident, composed, and editorial.

PHYSICS MATRIX:
- Signature phenomenon: polished-stone anisotropy and onyx-panel micro-reflection bifurcation synchronized with garment tension response.
- Force/load path: rail support -> shoulder set -> waist compression -> wrap tension gradient -> stance-driven knee stretch change.
- Mechanical tests that must pass:
- Onyx reflections must preserve straight-line continuity across panel seams and brass inlay.
- Stone highlights should show elongated anisotropy near the key-light path.
- Wrap overlap should tighten at waist and relax toward free hem without topology collapse.
- Hosiery translucency must vary with side-light angle and knee flexion state.

CAMERA + LIGHT SPEC:
- Camera rig: vertical 4:5, 50mm full-frame look, f/2.8, full-body with atrium depth.
- Lighting rig: warm lantern key against cool onyx-wall bounce with clean air and crisp contact shadows.
- Enforce coherent key/fill/rim causality and shadow direction discipline.

REJECT MATRIX:
- Reject if reflections break continuity across onyx seams or brass inlay.
- Reject if heel contact shadow disappears under hard key.
- Reject if body anatomy at hip/knee transitions is implausible.
- Reject if any liquid-contact or vapor-atmospheric effect appears.
- Reject any explicit framing, nudity, fetish coding, anatomy break, texture repetition, clipping, or plastic skin.
