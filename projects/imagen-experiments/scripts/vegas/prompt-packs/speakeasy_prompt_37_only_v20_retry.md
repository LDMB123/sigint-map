## Prompt 37 - Gallery Flashpoint

### Primary Prompt

MISSION:
Create an intimate, sultry, high-fashion nightlife frame with stronger daring attire, stronger real-life realism, stronger physics coherence, and deliberately sensual pose choreography with intimate framing pressure while remaining non-explicit.

NON-EXPLICIT LIMITS (MANDATORY):
- Adult subject only. No nudity, no explicit sexual content, no explicit framing.

PRIORITY ORDER (HARD):
1) Identity + direct eye contact.
2) Exact attire/colors and two-piece topology.
3) Physics realism and material realism.
4) Scene edge and composition aggression.

PHYSICS REALISM VALIDATOR (ALL MUST HOLD):
- Lighting physics: practical-light falloff follows inverse-square behavior; nearest surfaces clearly brighter than distant surfaces.
- Stability physics: center of mass reads inside support base; no floating or impossible load paths.
- Contact physics: visible contact compression at heels/seat/hand supports with coherent contact shadows.
- Cloth physics: slit opening follows pose chain, seam tension follows body torque, stocking denier/translucency and thigh-band compression are consistent.
- Material optics: skin has pore-scale texture and soft specular rolloff; metal has sharper highlights; satin/latex show directionally plausible sheen.
- Camera realism: physically plausible depth of field, micro-noise grain, no over-smoothed CGI look.

ANTI-ARTIFACT NEGATIVE LIST (PLAIN DESCRIPTORS):
- plastic skin, wax texture, airbrushed face, detached shadow, fake reflection, warped joints, fused garments, one-piece merge, all-black substitution, cartoon render.

COMPOSITION LOCK:
- Full body visible, 75-85% frame occupancy, direct lens contact, strong leading lines, selective foreground occlusion, intentional frame-edge pressure.
- Use one clear camera profile, one clear scene anchor set, and one clear pose chain.
- Sensual framing pressure: maintain eye-line dominance, shoulder-to-hip counter-rotation, and deliberate hand choreography (no static mannequin arms).
- Devil's-advocate guardrails: reject dead-hands, over-arched lumbar, impossible heel balance, floating limbs, or slit geometry that fights gravity.
- Edge/realism lock: include one foreground practical crossing frame edge, preserve natural asymmetry (no mannequin symmetry), and keep texture-level detail in skin, fabric weave, and reflections.

PROMPT CONCEPT:
- private art-opening lounge portrait with intimate tension.

SCENE ANCHORS (MANDATORY):
- dark gallery corridor, spotlighted art panels, champagne tray glints, suspended haze.
- Sultry environment lock: private-hour nightlife mood, low-key practicals with warm/cool contrast, no sterile daytime or generic lobby atmosphere.

ATTIRE (MANDATORY EXACT MATCH):
- jade cutout crop with plunging mesh bridge + ruby high-slit skirt with side cinch tension, black thigh-high stockings, pointed pumps.
- Styling edge lock: preserve daring cut lines and fabric tension (slit/mesh/cutout intent); never conservative-restyle or merge into one-piece topology.

POSE TARGET:
- long-stride freeze, pelvis leading, shoulder opposition, clutch hand weighted and realistic.
- Sensual pose lock: one weight-bearing leg plus one display leg, visible S-curve through ankle-hip-sternum, active fingers/jawline/core tension, direct-lens intimacy without explicit content.

KINEMATIC CHAIN:
- forward momentum -> shoulder counter-rotation -> skirt drag vector -> clutch inertia -> forefoot load -> COM capture.
- Anatomy guardrail: human-plausible joint ranges only; clear load path from support to pelvis to ribcage to gaze; no hyperextension or disconnected limb angles.

FRAMING BLUEPRINT:
- gallery vanishing lines with sculpture-edge foreground and side-light contouring.
- Intimacy lock: keep lens-to-subject distance in a close editorial band (about 1.8-2.6m), include foreground occlusion crossing 8-15% of frame, and keep camera height between upper-thigh and sternum unless scene logic requires otherwise.
- Frame pressure lock: occupy 82-88% of frame height with deliberate edge crop pressure at one shoulder and one heel while preserving full-body visibility.

CAMERA PROFILE:
- Canon R5, 40mm, f/1.8, 1/200s, ISO 1250, linear practicals + warm practical spill.

HARD REJECT:
- identity drift, off-camera gaze, weak physics realism, conservative downgrade, flat framing, all-black collapse, one-piece merge, explicit framing.
