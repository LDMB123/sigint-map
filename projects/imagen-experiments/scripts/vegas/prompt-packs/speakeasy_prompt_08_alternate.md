## Prompt 08 - Cellar Convergence Field

### Primary Prompt

MISSION:
Create a fashion-forward wine-cellar editorial portrait with strong realism and confident nightlife energy.

IDENTITY LOCK:
- Preserve exact reference identity and direct camera gaze.

SCENE ANCHORS:
- Glass wine cabinets, backlit bottles, polished concrete corridor, warm ambient pools.

ATTIRE UPGRADE (EDGY TWO-PIECE):
- Deep burgundy structured crop top with architectural seams + matching high-waist satin skirt with controlled side vent.
- Black sheer tights and burgundy pointed stilettos.

REALISM MICRODETAIL:
- Pore texture, natural lip microtexture, seam-thread continuity, realistic satin roughness rolloff.
- Bottle-glass reflections physically consistent with lens angle and light position.

PHYSICS / CAUSALITY:
- Walking motion -> hem inertia -> side-vent spread control -> torso counter-balance -> heel contact compression.

CAMERA + LIGHT:
- Vertical 4:5 full body.
- Warm practical key with low cool fill; cabinet reflections and shadows must remain physically consistent.

HARD REJECT:
- Identity drift, cabinet anchor loss, one-piece collapse, fake lace/texture artifacts, detached shadows.

### Safe Backup Prompt

MISSION:
Generate a safety-stable cellar variation while preserving identity and upscale edgy styling.

IDENTITY LOCK:
- Keep exact face and direct lens gaze.

SCENE ANCHORS:
- Keep wine cabinets, bottles, and concrete corridor lighting.

SAFE ATTIRE (EDGY/STABLE TWO-PIECE):
- Burgundy structured crop top + matching skirt with moderated side vent and stable seam lines.
- Black sheer tights and pointed heels.

REALISM MICRODETAIL:
- Preserve pores, seam continuity, and believable cabinet reflections.

PHYSICS / CAUSALITY:
- Maintain walking-induced garment response with stable edge behavior.

HARD REJECT:
- Identity drift, one-piece fallback, scene drift, over-smoothed skin, explicit framing.
