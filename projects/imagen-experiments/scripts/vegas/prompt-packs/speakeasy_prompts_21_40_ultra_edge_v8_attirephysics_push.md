IDENTITY LOCK:
- Match exact reference identity and direct lens engagement.
- Preserve facial geometry and hairline consistency across attempts.

CONTROVERSY VECTOR (ADULT, NON-EXPLICIT):
- Push provocative, anti-establishment couture language.
- Make the styling clearly daring, sensual, and controversial.
- Keep it non-explicit: no nudity, no explicit sexual framing.

- Strong S-curve posture with deliberate hip asymmetry and ribcage counter-rotation.
- One confident support leg and one presentation leg that lengthens the upper-thigh line.
- Controlled hand choreography that sculpts waist, hip, and torso lines.

ATTIRE EDGE ESCALATION (NON-EXPLICIT, MANDATORY):
- Use a clearly daring two-piece architecture: micro-crop proportion, deeper neckline geometry, stronger side-waist cut structure, open-back cues, and aggressive slit geometry.
- Increase visible skin in a fashion-editorial way while preserving coverage-critical zones.
- Keep silhouette tension high: sharp contour lines, assertive cut placement, and unmistakable provocative styling.

- Solve strap load paths: each strap must show realistic tension direction, anchor behavior, and pressure transfer to the body.
- Solve panel strain: contour panels must show physically plausible extension/compression gradients and seam stress concentration at pivot zones.

- Solve hosiery mechanics: denier translucency, welt-band compression, skin interaction, and shear behavior must look physically real.
- Solve wrinkle logic: fold frequency and orientation must follow material stiffness, cut direction, and local curvature.

- Biomechanics: center of mass inside support polygon; plausible joint limits and spinal curvature.
- Contact: heel-floor friction, foot-ground load transfer, skin-fabric compression gradients, hand-object reaction forces.
- Optics: shadow direction, reflection geometry, exposure balance, and highlight rolloff must be coherent.
- Materials: pore-scale skin detail, realistic subsurface scattering, non-repeating textile microtexture.

EXPERT COMPOSITION LOCK:
- Subject occupies 70-80% of frame height with full head-to-toe visibility.
- Use strong leading lines and clean depth layering.
- Keep perspective and vertical geometry physically coherent.

ANTI-SYNTHETIC LOCK:
- No plastic skin, no over-smoothed texture, no fake reflections, no impossible cloth behavior.

PROMPT-SPECIFIC CONTROVERSIAL CONCEPT:
- Concept: cathedral noir rebellion.

PROMPT-SPECIFIC POSE TARGET:
