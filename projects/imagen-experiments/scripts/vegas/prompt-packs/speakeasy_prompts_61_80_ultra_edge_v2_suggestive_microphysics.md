## Prompt 80 - Midnight Skyline Finale

### Primary Prompt

MISSION:
Create a dramatically upgraded intimate, sultry, high-fashion nightlife frame with slightly more suggestive (still non-explicit) styling, stronger pose chemistry, and a 50% uplift target in edge, realism, and physics.

NON-EXPLICIT LIMITS (MANDATORY):
- Adult subject only. No nudity, no explicit sexual content, no explicit framing.

PRIORITY ORDER (HARD):
1) Identity + direct eye contact.
2) Exact attire/colors and two-piece topology.
3) Micro-physics realism and material realism.
4) Suggestive scene tension + composition aggression.

PHYSICS REALISM VALIDATOR (ALL MUST HOLD):
- Lighting physics: practical-light falloff follows inverse-square behavior; nearest surfaces clearly brighter than distant surfaces.
- Stability physics: center of mass reads inside support base; no floating or impossible load paths.
- Contact physics: visible contact compression at heels/seat/hand supports with coherent contact shadows.
- Cloth physics: slit opening follows pose chain, seam tension follows body torque, stocking denier/translucency and thigh-band compression are consistent.
- Material optics: skin has pore-scale texture and soft specular rolloff; metal has sharper highlights; satin/latex show directionally plausible sheen.
- Camera realism: physically plausible depth of field, micro-noise grain, no over-smoothed CGI look.

MICRO-PHYSICS EXPERT STACK (MANDATORY ALL HOLD):
- Wrinkle hierarchy: macro gravity folds, meso seam-driven buckling, micro creases in high-strain zones.
- Fabric anisotropy: directional highlight flow and bias-direction drape differences must read naturally.
- Top/skirt coupling: phase-lag motion between top hem and waistband; never fused one-piece behavior.
- Hosiery strain map: denier-aware translucency gradient cuff-to-toe with realistic cuff pressure transition.
- Hand/foot load cues: tendon, knuckle, and instep tension must match support force.
- Surface interaction: contact compression on seats/rails and roughness-aware reflections on glass/metal/stone.
- Reflection integrity: no detached reflections; angle and roughness govern intensity and blur.
- Sensor authenticity: subtle shadow grain and natural highlight rolloff, no plastic smoothing.

ANTI-ARTIFACT NEGATIVE LIST (PLAIN DESCRIPTORS):
- plastic skin, wax texture, airbrushed face, detached shadow, fake reflection, warped joints, fused garments, one-piece merge, all-black substitution, cartoon render.

COMPOSITION LOCK:
- Full body visible, 82-90% frame occupancy, direct lens contact, strong leading lines, selective foreground occlusion, intentional frame-edge pressure.
- Use one clear camera profile, one clear scene anchor set, and one clear pose chain.
- Suggestive framing pressure: maintain eye-line dominance, shoulder-to-hip counter-rotation, and deliberate hand choreography (no static mannequin arms).
- Devil's-advocate guardrails: reject dead-hands, over-arched lumbar, impossible heel balance, floating limbs, or slit geometry that fights gravity.
- Edge lock: include at least one practical-light foreground crossing 8-15% of frame.

PROMPT CONCEPT:

SCENE ANCHORS (MANDATORY):

- Sultry environment lock: private-hour nightlife mood, low-key practicals with warm/cool contrast, no sterile daytime or generic lobby atmosphere.
