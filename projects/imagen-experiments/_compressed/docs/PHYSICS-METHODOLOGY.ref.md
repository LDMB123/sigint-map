## Key Formulas

**Camera:**
- Airy disc radius: r = 1.22 × λ × f-number = 1.22 × 0.00055mm × 1.78 = 1.2μm
- DOF: DOF = (2 × N × C × d²) / f² where N=f/1.78, C=circle of confusion

**Light Transport:**
- Beer-Lambert: I(x) = I₀ × e^(-μ×x) (absorption through medium)
- Kubelka-Munk 2-flux: SSS in skin, (1-R)²/2R = K/S ratio

**Lens:**
- Zernike polynomials: Z_n^m(ρ,θ) for aberrations (coma, spherical, astigmatism)
