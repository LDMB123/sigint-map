## Technical Implementation Notes

### Photorealism Shield (600+ words)

**Proven Effective Components:**
- Camera capture physics: iPhone 15 Pro, specific ISO/aperture/shutter, sensor noise profile
- Lens characteristics: Chromatic aberration, vignetting, bokeh artifacts, focus breathing
- Material physics: Fabric drape, light absorption/reflection, texture detail
- Skin microstructure: Pore detail, sebaceous filaments, broken capillaries, fine lines
- Lighting physics: Color temperature (Kelvin), light transport, specular vs diffuse

**Usage:** Prepend to every concept, accounting for ~400 of 600-word minimum

**Tootsie's Orchid Lounge:**

**Robert's Western World:**

**Acme Feed & Seed:**

### Hosiery Specification Language (Proven Safe)

**Pattern:** "[Type] [denier] [pattern] thigh-highs with [band width] lace top band visible where hem ends"

**Examples:**
- "Sheer black 10-denier thigh-highs with 3-inch lace top band visible where hem ends"
- "Fishnet thigh-highs with diamond pattern, elastic top creating faint line beneath dress hem"
- "Sheer nude 12-denier Cuban heel thigh-highs, 2.5-inch darker reinforcement visible"

**Safe Language:** Passive visibility ("visible where hem ends") vs active exposure ("showing thigh-highs")
