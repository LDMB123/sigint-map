# CONSTRAINT-BASED PHOTOREALISM

## The Anti-Plastic Approach

Instead of describing effects, we enforce LIMITATIONS and IMPERFECTIONS that real cameras CANNOT avoid:

---

**MANDATORY CAMERA LIMITATIONS - UNRETOUCHED RAW FILE:**

This is an UNEDITED, UNRETOUCHED camera RAW file converted to PNG with ZERO post-processing. NO beauty filters, NO skin smoothing, NO AI enhancement, NO clarity/texture adjustments, NO sharpening beyond lens MTF, NO noise reduction.

**SENSOR CAPTURED EXACTLY AS-IS:**
- Skin texture: Every visible pore (0.05-0.2mm diameter), sebaceous filaments, peach fuzz catching light, fine wrinkles, uneven skin tone, minor blemishes, oil shine on T-zone, slight redness
- Fabric reality: Individual thread weave visible, pulled threads, pilling, wrinkles with shadow depth, lint particles, color variation in dye, worn edges, stretched areas
- Hair chaos: Frizz, flyaways, split ends catching light differently, natural grease near scalp, dust particles, individual strands separating, root line showing, uneven curl pattern

**CHEAP LENS LIMITATIONS (NOT pro-grade optics):**
- Soft corners from field curvature - edges 20% less sharp than center
- Chromatic aberration at ALL high-contrast edges - visible color fringing
- Vignetting - natural darkening in corners (not added in post)
- Slight geometric distortion - straight lines slightly curved
- Lower contrast from lens flare/internal reflections near lights
- Visible coma on point light sources in background

**HIGH ISO NOISE (NOT clean image):**
- ISO 1600-3200 grain throughout entire image
- Color noise in shadows (red/green speckles)
- Luminance grain texture visible on all surfaces
- Blue channel noisier than red/green
- Noise MORE visible in smooth areas (walls, skin, fabric)

**IMPERFECT EXPOSURE:**
- NOT perfectly exposed - either slight overexposure (blown highlights in windows/lights) OR underexposure (crushed shadow detail)
- Mixed color temperatures creating color casts - warm tungsten vs cool window light
- Harsh shadows with NO fill light (natural light falloff)
- Specular highlights clipping to pure white
- Deep shadows going to pure black (lost detail)

**REAL LIGHTING PROBLEMS:**
- Inverse square falloff creating dramatic light drop-off
- Hard shadows with sharp edges (NOT soft studio lighting)
- Color temperature mismatch - orange tungsten vs blue daylight creating skin color shifts
- Underlit areas going muddy/noisy
- Bright areas showing halation/bloom
- No perfect three-point lighting - this is AVAILABLE LIGHT only

**IMPERFECT FOCUS:**
- Focus NOT perfect across entire subject
- Shallow depth of field at f/1.8-2.8 - only narrow plane sharp
- Background significantly out of focus (NOT slightly soft - actually blurred)
- Slight front/back focus error - not perfectly on the eyes
- Focus breathing causing slight perspective shift

**MOTION ARTIFACTS:**
- Slight motion blur from 1/60s shutter (hands, hair)
- Camera shake micro-blur (handheld, not tripod)
- Subject micro-movement during exposure
- Image NOT perfectly sharp due to vibration

**COMPRESSION & DIGITAL ARTIFACTS:**
- This is a JPEG/PNG conversion from RAW - compression artifacts visible
- Slight banding in gradients (8-bit color depth limits)
- Edge ringing from compression
- Posterization in smooth gradients

**WHAT THIS IS NOT:**
- NOT retouched portrait
- NOT studio lighting
- NOT professional makeup
- NOT styled hair
- NOT color graded
- NOT sharpened beyond optical limits
- NOT noise reduced
- NOT skin smoothed
- NOT magazine quality
- NOT Instagram filtered

**THIS IS:**
- Handheld camera in dim bar
- Available light only
- Consumer/prosumer lens (NOT $2000+ pro glass)
- High ISO (grain visible)
- No post-processing
- No makeup artist
- No hair stylist
- No retouching
- Real person in real environment
- Unedited reality

**TECHNICAL CAPTURE PARAMETERS:**
Must exhibit ALL of these technical limitations:
- ISO 1600-3200 (NOT clean ISO 100)
- f/1.8-2.8 (NOT f/8 deep DOF)
- 1/60-1/125s (NOT stabilized long exposure)
- Available light (NOT studio strobes)
- Handheld (NOT tripod stable)
- 35-50mm lens (NOT portrait 85mm compression)
- JPEG compression (NOT lossless)
- 8-bit color (NOT 16-bit smooth gradients)

**THE PLASTIC PROBLEM:**
AI generates "perfect" images because it's trained on retouched, processed, magazine-quality photos. This MUST look like:
- A phone camera snapshot in dim lighting
- Zero retouching budget
- Available light only
- Real person (not model with professional makeup)
- Authentic candid moment (not posed editorial)

If skin looks smooth/plastic, if fabric looks too clean, if lighting looks too perfect, if noise is absent, if edges are too sharp - IT'S FAILED and looks AI-generated.

## Usage

This is a CONSTRAINT ENFORCEMENT prefix. Add to every prompt, then describe the scene.

Example:
```
[ENTIRE CONSTRAINT PREFIX ABOVE]

Shot on iPhone 14 Pro at ISO 2500, f/1.8, 1/60s. Woman at dive bar in emerald dress. Direct eye contact. Warm tungsten overhead, cool window light from left creating color temperature mismatch on her face...
```
