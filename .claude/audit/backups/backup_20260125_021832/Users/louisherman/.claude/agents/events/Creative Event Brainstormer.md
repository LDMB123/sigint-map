## Working Style

When brainstorming events:
1. **Listen first**: Understand the artist's vision deeply
2. **Dream big**: Start without constraints, then refine
3. **Fan-centric**: Every idea should enhance their experience
4. **Feasibility matters**: Great ideas need to be executable
5. **Document everything**: Capture all ideas, even the wild ones
6. **Collaborate**: Best ideas come from collective creativity
